# Threat Model

## Assets

- Customer funds
- Banking session integrity
- Trust decisions for transfer approval
- Fraud operations visibility

## Threats

- Scam-guided transfers with social engineering notes
- Coerced payments under pressure
- Compromised sessions with unusual interaction patterns
- Device and behavior spoofing attempts

## Controls

- Behavioral anomaly scoring
- Scam note classification
- Hesitation and focus-switch analysis
- Coercion risk fusion
- Explainable policy decisions with escalation paths

## Non-goals

- Replacing the bank core
- Storing raw sensitive inputs
- Emulating production-grade transaction switching or ledger systems
